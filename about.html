'WHO AM I ?'


I'm Leon, a designer/front end developer orginally from London, now residing in Phoenix AZ. My journey as a creative has taken me half way around the world, kept me fascinated with learning, overflowing with creativity, and a drive to create something amazing no matter the medium. I love combining design, typography, colors, photography, and illustration with digital technology.



I began my career as a graphic designer/artist, creating product designs and artwork for such notable clients as Harley Davidson, Indian Motorcycle, and Victory/Polaris. My designs have been featured in multiple national magazines, such as Street Trucks, Urban Bagger, Hot Bike magazine, and National TV shows like Discovery Channels Biker Build off. After many enjoyable years, I grew into new challenges and jumped headlong into web development.

Fast forward to present day, and you will find me focusing on utilizing my strengths in design to create pixel perfect UI Designs, work with UX Processes, align with buisness objectives, research, usability test, wireframe and prototype to solve problems, and develop the web with beautiful semantic HTML/CSS and Javascript. Utilize my knowledge of front end frameworks like twitter bootstrap as well as CMS systems like wordpress with one goal... build great user centered experiences.
