Zushi Resturant, based in Mesa, Arizona has been in buisness for 7 years. A well established Sushi and Japanese Bistro that has recently expanded to offer Tappanyaki to there local customers.


As a result of their expansion and new remodel they were in need of an online prescense that accurrately portrays their brand, connects with customers and provides a better user experience. The new website was designed to incorporate modern features such as a online reservation system, and use of the google map API.

SITE BEFORE

Client has an existing site that lacked functionality, had poor User experience, no brand strategy. It failed to Improve Brand Awareness.

The site failed to connect with the client base with social media, for social proofing. The images were not professional and the site failed to increase sales by not offering online booking of the Tappanyaki resturant

Wireframes and Prototypes

Given aspects as the content of the site, the business goals and several usabilty reviews wireframes were designed in Sketch for the user interaction and flow.

Visual Proposal

A high fidelity mockup was created in photoshop in order to acomplish the website objectives



OUTCOME & FUTURE CONSIDERATIONS

The redesign was well received within the company, this opened up the owner to expanding the scope of the build to a future iteration with a backend. As a whole very happy with the site has come and is moving towards hitting the customers goals and more.
